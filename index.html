Welcome to Madagascar 🇲🇬

History

Madagascar was first settled by Austronesian voyagers over 2,000 years ago. It later became a unified Kingdom in the 19th century before becoming a French colony, regaining independence in 1960.

Major Cities

Antananarivo: The bustling capital city in the central highlands.

Toamasina: The country's most important port on the east coast.

Unique Wildlife 🐒

Lemur

Lemurs are unique primates found only in Madagascar.

Fossa

The fossa is the top predator of the island.

Chameleon

Chameleons can change color and live in trees.

Geography & Climate

Madagascar is the fourth-largest island in the world. It has a tropical climate, with a hot, rainy season (November to April) and a cooler, dry season (May to October).

Football: The Barea

The national team is known as "The Barea." They made history in 2019 by reaching the quarter-finals of the Africa Cup of Nations!

Culture & Cuisine

Madagascan food is delicious! Try Romazava (meat stew) or Ravitoto (pork with crushed cassava leaves).
