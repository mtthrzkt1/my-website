Welcome to Madagascar 🇲🇬

History

Madagascar was first settled by Austronesian voyagers over 2,000 years ago. Later, the Kingdom of Madagascar unified the island in the 19th century before becoming a French colony. It finally regained independence in 1960.

Major Cities

Antananarivo: The bustling capital city in the central highlands.

Toamasina: The country's most important port on the east coast.

Antsirabe: Known for its thermal baths and colonial architecture.

Unique Wildlife 🐒

Lemur

Lemurs are found only in Madagascar.

Fossa

The fossa is the top predator of Madagascar.

Chameleon

Chameleons can change color and live in trees.
